Brake Tests

BrakeSafe (Spongebob)

  1. Turn on the brakesafe by pressing RESET, wait for the display to show BRAKESAFE READY with a time and date
  2. If you have a foot pedal airpad ensure it is enabled for service brake testing (see operating instructions)
  3. Position the brakesafe in the vehicle, position in the direction of travel and preferably against the bulkhead
  4. press A, the display will show armed for test #. use up and down arrows to change test number. press the ENTER button to confirm selection
  5. The display will now show Vehicle ID, use the up and down arrows to enter a vehicle ID (numerous IDs can be stored or downlkoaded via a PC). to change the ID see o verleaf editing IDs. once completed press and hold ENTER for two beeps
  6. the display will now show the trailer ID, use the same principal as the vehicle ID to enter trailer ID. once completed press and hold ENTER for two beeps
  7. the display will now show GET SLOPE OFFSET, position the vehicle at the start of the test run and then press ENTER
  8. The display will now show dont name brake. use the up and down arrows to alternate through service brake, parking brake and secondary brake, these will appear on the test print out. press ENTER
  9. The display will now show [ENTER] before accelerating, check for a safe test area, once confirmed press ENTER
  10. The display will show armed for test # with the speed at 0Kpm/Mph
  11. Accelerate the vehicle at a constant rate beyond 10mph and apply an emergency top braking procedure
  12. once stopped hold the vehicle on the brake until a result has appeared on the brakesafe display
  13. press PRINT to immediately print off the brake test
  14. alternatively press A to rearm and go to step 8
